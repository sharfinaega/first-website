About Us

HEY PIZZA started as a single store in 1970 by Tom and his brother Jerry as The Pizza. When Tom became the sole owner of the business, He changed the name to HEY PIZZA Inc. HEY PIZZA is the recognized world leader in pizza delivery, with a significant. Business in carryout pizza. Today HEY PIZZA ranks among the world's top public restaurant barnds with its global enterprise of more than 15.000 stores in 75 international markets. In August 2007, HEY PIZZA opened the first store in Indonesia at Pondok Indah. HEY PIZZA currently has operated in more than 100 store in 5 cities of indonesia includes Jakarta, Bandung, Yogyakarta, Surabaya, an Bogor.
